## đźš€ Quick Start: How to Edit the Site

Follow these 4 simple steps to update your website:

### Step 1: Open the Editor
* Open your web browser and go to your admin link (e.g., `https://admin.godlinkagency.com` or double-click the `admin.html` file provided to you).
* A prompt will ask for your **Admin Password**. Type your password to unlock the ability to make changes.

### Step 2: Choose What to Change
* Look at the **Left Sidebar**. You will see a list of sections (like **Basics**, **Hero**, **Properties**, etc.).
* Click on any section to display its contents in the center of your screen.

### Step 3: Make Your Edits
* **Text**: Simply click inside any box and start typing.
* **Colors**: Click on any color box to open a color palette window and select a new shade.
* **Single Images**: Drag an image file from your computer and drop it directly into the dashed box, or click the **Select image from PC** button to select a file.
* **Galleries & Videos**: For multiple listing photos or video tours, you can upload files from your PC or paste web links directly into the designated boxes.

### Step 4: Save Your Changes
* When you are finished editing, click the green **Save** button in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar.
* **Done!** The changes are sent directly to the site. If you have the live website open in another tab, your changes will update instantly without even needing a manual page refresh.

Here is a breakdown of what you can change in each sidebar section and where those changes appear on the live website:

### 1. Basics (General Information)
This section controls the general identity and footer of the agency site.
* **Website Name & Tagline**: Updates the name displayed in the top header and bottom footer.
* **Logo Image**: Upload or update your company logo. 
* **Contact Details**: Change the email address, phone number, and brief description that visitors use to contact you.
* **Change Admin Login**: Use this button if you need to set a new admin password.

### 2. Pages (SEO & Google Search Settings)
This section helps search engines like Google understand your pages.
* **Page Title**: The name displayed on the browser tab (e.g., *Home â€“ God Link Agency*).
* **Page Description**: The short summary description shown underneath your site name in Google search results. Keep this concise and professional.

### 3. Theme (Website Style & Colors)
Change the overall design, colors, and layout look.
* **Primary**: The main highlight accent color (e.g., buttons, borders, active tags).
* **Background / Paper**: The background colors for pages and sections (light/dark modes).
* **Text / Ink**: Colors used for main headings and descriptions.

### 4. Hero (First Impression Banner)
This represents the very first banner visitors see when they open the homepage.
* **Main Headline**: The large bold text at the top of the home page.
* **Description**: The summary statement underneath the headline.
* **Stats**: Add numeric key performance details (e.g., `150+ Happy Clients` or `12+ Years Experience`), typing **one stat per line**.
* **Hero Image**: The primary background image that fits behind your main text.

### 5. Reasons (Trust Cards)
Short descriptive cards on your homepage highlighting why clients should choose God Link Agency.
* **Icon or short label**: A small identifier (e.g., a simple word or icon tag).
* **Title & Description**: Headline and body text for each trust point.
* *Controls*: Use **Duplicate** to clone a card, **Delete** to remove it, and **Add new** to create a fresh trust point.

### 6. Categories (Listing Filters)
Control the property types that visitors use to filter listings.
* Add or remove categories like `Residential`, `Commercial`, `Land`, or `Rentals`.
* Visitors will see these as filter tabs on the Listings page.

### 7. Properties (Your Listings Manager)
This is where you manage your real estate directory.
* **Details**: Enter the listing price, location, status (e.g., `Available`, `Sold`), and specifications (bedrooms, bathrooms, area size, parking spaces).
* **Main Image**: The single primary image shown on the grid cards.
* **Gallery Images**: Add extra photos. You can select **multiple files at once** from your computer to upload them, or paste public links.
* **Videos**: Drag and drop a walk-through video tour (`.mp4` format) to let visitors view the property dynamically.
* **Features**: List property perks (e.g., `Private Pool`, `Air Conditioning`, `Modern Kitchen`) typing **one feature per line**.

## đź’ˇ Pro-Tips for Managing Content

* **Always Have a Backup**: Before making a large batch of listing edits, you can click the **Download** button in the sidebar footer. This saves a backup copy of your current site data to your computer.
* **Resetting Mistakes**: If you make a mistake while editing and have not clicked **Save** yet, click the **Reload** button at the top right. This cancels your current unsaved session and restores the live version.
* **Image Optimization**: To keep your website running fast, try to upload images under 2MB in size. Extremely large photos (e.g. direct files from professional cameras) might slow down the page loading time for your visitors.
* **Video Formats**: For the video walk-through feature, use compressed `.mp4` files for the best loading speed.
